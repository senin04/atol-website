Coingi the Exchange

Professional grade Cryptocurrency Exchange and Exchange Engine allows hundreds of thousands of people to trade at the same time from any part of the world. Developed since 2014 and launched in 2017 with a strong focus on scalability and security.

Exchange as a service

Coingi provides full API, so you can easily start your complete institutional-grade ecosystem. Coingi provides a full-stack solution that allows exchange operators to quickly develop profitable revenue streams.

No scalability limits

Designed as a massive, unstoppable infrastructure, allowing hundreds of thousands of users to be served at the same time. Fully scaled, running from dozens of servers spread across multiple sites.

Bank grade security

We have designed and developed Coingi with best security practices and implemented a multi-layer security architecture, making the exchange virtually impenetrable. 0% of customers’ funds lost since the start is the best proof.
