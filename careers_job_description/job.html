Rust Developer

This is a big challenge, but it’s also a unique opportunity to work with our talented team developing quality systems that will shape the future of a multi-billion dollar industry. If you know Linux and know RUST, we want you!
Apply Other positions

What awaits
You here?

You will work mainly on high-frequency arbitrage bots and surrounding tools. However, we do not require any further knowledge of trading and market arbitrage from you.

You will be primarily working on an extremely optimised trading bot working around the clock while moving loads of BTC, ETH, USDT, etc. all-round the world.

As a Rust developer, you could join our most important project HF Arbitrage Bot and/or be part of our new upcoming projects :)

More about work in ATOL

Positon
Requirements

Experience with Rust language – ideally with multiple (completed) projects in Rust or with a contribution to the language library itself.

Deep experience with processing high loads of data.

To be able to work in a team as well as on its own.

Ability to work well under pressure in a fast-developing environment.

Be accountable for the quality of your work.

Don't be overzealous, but rather be calm :).

We appreciate your experience with Redis and Time-critical processes. The knowledge of async/await parts of Rust (especially to the Tokio framework) is also beneficial.

What we
Offer to you

Full-time position with flexible working hours and the possibility to work fully remote.

Indefinite agreement.

Both possibilities to work as an Employee or Freelance.

Unlimited PTO (If the work is done).

Company events, regular brunches and snack bar.

Relax room, shower, room for bicycles and parking spot.

Bonus for self-education.

HW equipment according to your wishes.

Relocation package.

Modern offices by the Metro B in Prague, Czech Republic — the heart of Europe.

Work on long-term projects in an interesting area of ​​cryptocurrencies, fintech and trading.

Location

We’re looking for skilled developers to be part of our international team. You can work remotely, or join us at our headquarters in Prague, Czech Republic — the heart of Europe. We are happy to accommodate your preferences.

We will love you
Even more if

You are interested in cryptocurrencies or have active experience with them.

You are interested in new technologies and development procedures.

You have a sense of humour :)

Instead of a CV, you can send your LinkedIn profile. And if you are developing an open-source project, we would like to take a look.
