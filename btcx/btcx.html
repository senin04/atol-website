Bitcoin Exchange network

A dynamically growing Bitcoin exchange and ATMs are a gateway to the world of cryptocurrencies. The service targets beginners and more advanced users who want to buy Bitcoin easily and securely. The exchange operates as an API service and thus can be seen online under several independent brand names.

Security & Antifraud protection

The system evaluates many metrics on every trade to detect all potential frauds. The security control stops anything suspicious, asks the clients for additional verification or request manual checks if needed. The system is among the top in its field, not only on the "Czech Internet".

ATMs

As a complement to the online exchange, we also operate Cryptocurrency ATMs that allow you to buy sell cryptocurrencies for cash and support the brand.

Online Bitcoin Exchange

The Bitcoin exchange btctex.cz has been operating since 2020. Providing maximum simplicity and speed of transactions without the need for complex registration or installation of mobile applications.

The cryptocurrency exchange uses the Coingi.com exchange for receiving, sending and trading, which provides complete security and backend for handling cryptocurrencies.

The exchange provides APIs for traffic partners, for which it technically arranges trades and operates under the brand or co-brand of the partner. This allows partners to expand their portfolio of services for their users and better monetize the traffic of their website or mobile app.
